# **Module 6 - Citation Extractor**
## **Responsibilities**
- Detect in-text citations appearing in the paper (numeric, author-year, bracketed, or inline forms).  
- Extract citation markers (e.g., “[12]”, “Vaswani et al., 2017”).  
- Group citations by the section in which they appear.  

## **Outputs**
For each section:
- A list of citation markers present in the end of each section
- If no citation in the section, put {no citiation} in the end.
